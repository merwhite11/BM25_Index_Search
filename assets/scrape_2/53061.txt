What do you call the opposite of a leap second?

A second is sometimes added to compensate for the slowing down of the Earth's rotation, these are called "leap seconds" by analogy with "leap years". There has been a decision to abandon leap seconds sometime before 2035, and moreover the Earth is now spinning faster, meaning that there may be a need to do the opposite of adding a second, that is skipping a second.
